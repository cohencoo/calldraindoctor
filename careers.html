LICENSED PLUMBER

Position Summary



Plumbers will be responsible for assembling, installing, and repairing pipes, fittings, fixtures of heating, water, and drainage systems according to specifications and plumbing codes.

Duties/Responsibilities



  • Analyze building plans and working drawings to determine work aids required on a project and its sequence of installations
  • Inspect structures to avoid obstructions that may form as a result of the installation of piping
  • Locate and mark positions of pipe, pipe connections, and passage holes using appropriate hand tools
  • Cuts openings in walls and floors to accommodate pipe and pipe fittings using hand tools and power tools.
  • Cuts and threads pipe using pipe cutters, cutting torch and pipe threading machine.
    • Bends pipe to required angle by use of pipe-bending machine or by placing pipe over block and bending it by hand.
  • Installs and assembles valves, pipe fittings, and pipes using a variety of piping metal (iron, steel, copper, etc.) and nonmetal (glass, vitrified clay, plastic, etc.) materials, using hand and power tools


    • Duties/Responsibilities Cont.



  • Joins pipes by use of screws, bolts, fittings, solder, plastic solvent, and caulks joints Fills pipe system with water or air and reads pressure gauges to determine whether system is leaking
  • Installs and repairs plumbing fixtures such as sinks, commodes, bathtubs, water heaters, hot water tanks, garbage disposal units, dishwashers, and water softeners
  • Repair and maintain plumbing as needed by replacing washers in leaky faucets, mending burst pipes and opening clogged drains.
  • Gather/collate/classify information
  • Attend to work assignment instructions or to supervisor’s orders
  • Exercise critical thinking and reasoning skills
  • Execute work-place safety regulations


Qualifications



  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • 4 years of prior plumbing experience
  • Driver’s License
  • Competencies
  • Teamwork
  • Work Environment/Safety
  • Accepting Direction
  • Delivering High Quality Work

Travel



Travel is frequently required to and from job site locations.

PLUMBER HELPER



Position Summary



The Plumber’s Helper will be responsible for helping plumbers assemble, install, and repair pipes, fittings, and fixtures of heating, water, and drainage systems according to supervisors’ direction.

Duties/Responsibilities



  • Assist in the installation of pipes and pipe fittings in accordance with regulation guidelines and using a variety of hand and power tools.
  • Physically cuts openings in walls and floor to accommodate pipe and pipe fittings using hand and power tools
  • Cuts and threads pipe using pipe cutters, cutting torch, and pipe threading machine
    • Installs repetitive hangers and supports for pipe and fixtures
  • Assembles and installs, under supervision, valves, pipe fittings, and pipes composed of a variety of piping metal (iron, steel, copper, etc.) and nonmetal (glass, vitrified clay, plastic, etc.) materials using a variety of hand and power tools
  • Attend to work assignment instructions or to supervisor’s orders
  • Duties/Responsibilities Cont.
  • Exercise critical thinking and reasoning skills
  • Execute work-place safety regulations


Qualifications



  • 3 to 6 months of prior plumbing experience
  • Driver’s License
  • High school level mathematics and English language skills


Competencies

  • Ownership and Accountability
  • Exceptionalism in all we do
  • Dependability
  • Customer Service
  • Accepting Direction
  • Technical Skills
  • Performing Physical or Mechanical Tasks
